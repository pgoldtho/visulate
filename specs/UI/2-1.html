Developer Notes

Records details of agreement between two or more parties to rent a building or a unit in a building. Agreements are typically fixed length 12 month agreements or month to month. A discount may be applied for prompt payment. Alternatively, a late fee could be charged if a payment id overdue. The discount amount column records the value of this charge and the discount type identifies the type Fee or Discount. Tenants may be required to pay a security deposit and last months rent as part of the agreement.

Some tenants have part or all of their rent paid by the government under a program called "Section 8". Low income tenants can apply to Section 8 for rent assistance. The program is administered from local Section 8 Offices. For example, there are 2 offices in the part of Florida where we have properties. Some tenants are registered with the Merritt Island office and others are registered with the Titusville office. A tenant can be registered with one and only one office.

The office issues a voucher to the tenant. This determines the maximum amount of rent they can pay for a property. For example, a tenant with a voucher for $800 may rent a property with a monthly rent of up to $800 but no more than that. Most Section 8 tenants are required to make a contribution towards their rent. For example a tenant with a voucher for $800 may be required to contribute $200 themselves. The landlord collects $200 from the tenant and the remainder from the government.

The screen should provide a quick view and data entry for tenant details. Basic mandatory fields and should be insertable from this screen. The View/Add Notes button should take the user to the Tenants page where additional details can be viewed, added and updated.
