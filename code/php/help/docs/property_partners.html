Business Partners

The Business Partners screen allows business owners to share information with other Visulate users. The screen has 2 sub pages. The Partner Search sub page is accessed by pressing the find link. It allows business owners to find users with a visulate account and grant access to a business unit. Enter the user's email address in the email address field then press the find button. This will present a list of existing partner assignments for that user and allow you to add new ones.

The Partners sub-page is the opening page. It shows contact and business unit assignment details for business partners. Visulate allows role based access to a business units. Each user can be granted access to a business unit based on their role. For example, a user may own a number of properties that they manage themself and also manage properites for other people.

In the example above 3 property owners and 3 property managers need to colaborate on the management of a number of properties. Each person needs to access the details for the properties they own or manage and should not be able to access other properties.

      Manager 1 needs to access Buildings A, B, E and G
      Manager 2 needs to access Building C
      Manager 3 needs to access Buildings F, H, and I
      Owner 1 needs to access Buildings A, B, C and D
      Owner 2 needs to access Buildings E, G, F and H
      Owner 3 needs to access Buildings I and J

To resolve this each owner would create a business unit for the properties that they own and then grant manager access to the property managers that need to access information about the buildings.
